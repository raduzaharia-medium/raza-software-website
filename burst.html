Bursts are detected and grouped automatically when you import a folder. No tagging, no manual work. The app reads the burst identifier from the photo metadata and groups the frames silently. In the photo grid, a burst appears as a single thumbnail — the first frame, with a small indicator. You won't see 40 copies of the same shot side by side. While navigating a burst, the current frame number and total frame count are shown so you always know where you are inside the sequence.
