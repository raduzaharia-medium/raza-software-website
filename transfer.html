Transfer is off by default. Grant Raza Photos access to your photo library, then flip on Allow photo transfer in Permissions — the same screen where you control read-only mode for the device.

  • Photo library access requested once, up front
  • Allow photo transfer gates sending from this device entirely
  • Read-only mode and transfer permission are independent switches

Select photos in the grid and tap the transfer icon to open the Transfer sheet. Every Mac on your network with incoming transfers enabled shows up automatically — the same Bonjour discovery that powers browsing. How discovery works →

  • Destination list is discovered on your network, no addresses to enter
  • Auto-transfer sends new camera roll shots the moment the app is open
  • Optionally delete originals from the device once a transfer completes

On the Mac, Transfer settings control the receiving side: a single toggle to allow incoming transfers, a destination folder, and how incoming files are organized once they arrive.

  • Allow incoming transfers is off until you turn it on
  • Pick any folder as the destination — it becomes part of your library like any other
  • Automatic year / month subfolders keep incoming photos organized
  • Original filenames are always preserved
